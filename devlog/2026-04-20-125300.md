## Investigating and Fixing the BulkExternalAdd Sync Regression

**Context:** After fixing the property-wipe bug in `prepare_update`, the Full-variant PBT revealed a secondary issue: 5 blocks from `BulkExternalAdd` transitions were missing from SQL after a 10-second timeout. Investigation traced this to a TOCTOU race in the file-sync loop.

**What happened:**

- Added eprintln tracing to `OrgSyncController::on_file_changed` to log every invocation, the blocks it saw in each diff, and timing.
- Ran PBT with 50 cases; identified the exact sequence: `BulkExternalAdd` writes 5 blocks (`bulk-14-{0..4}`) to disk while a previous `on_file_changed` (from `bulk-6`) was still mid-CDC-catchup and re-rendering the org file from cache.
- **Root cause**: After the handler processed `bulk-6`, it rendered the org tree from the cache (which hadn't yet incorporated `bulk-14`), then wrote that stale content back to disk, **silently overwriting the concurrent external write**. The next FSEvent saw disk matched `last_projection`, suppressed the echo, and `bulk-14` was lost.
- Fixed with a **read-before-write guard**: before writing rendered content back to disk, re-read the file. If disk changed since we parsed it, skip the write and update `last_projection` to the original parsed content so the next `on_file_changed` picks up the delta.

**Challenges:**

- The parallel session's `LoroShareBackend` factory in `loro_module.rs:282` was sync but called async-only `TursoEventBus::resolve()`. Fixed by switching to `try_resolve_async` and storing a future for later await.
- Tight timing window made the race hard to see without detailed tracing — required logging FSEvent sequence, parse timestamps, and cache-vs-disk timestamps.

**Decisions:**

- Chose read-before-write over locking because the file is external (no lock available) and the race is acceptable to lose—the guard just defers the echo to the next cycle.
- Kept all tracing as eprintln (not behind feature gates) to preserve visibility during PBT runs.

**Status:** TOCTOU guard applied and PBT re-running to confirm all 50 cases pass without the bulk-add timeout.
