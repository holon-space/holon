## Resolved WindowParams struct mismatch after rebasing holon branch

After rebasing the `holon` fork to the latest upstream Zed, a compilation error appeared in `gpui_macos` due to a divergence in the `WindowParams` struct definition between the fork and upstream.

**What happened**

- Identified `E0027` pattern matching error in `gpui_macos/src/window.rs:618` where destructuring `WindowParams` was missing the `icon` field
- Traced root cause: the fork's `gpui` crate added `icon: Option<Arc<RgbaImage>>` to `WindowParams`, but the `[patch]` directive in `Cargo.toml` only covered `gpui` and `gpui_wgpu`
- Upstream `gpui_macos` at rev `56104fb` was being compiled against the patched fork's `gpui`, creating a struct mismatch
- Verified the fork's own `gpui_macos` already handles this with `..` pattern syntax
- Extended the `[patch."https://github.com/zed-industries/zed"]` section to redirect all platform crates: `gpui_macos`, `gpui_linux`, `gpui_windows`, `gpui_web`, and `gpui_platform` to the `holon` branch
- Ran `cargo check -p holon-gpui` to verify the fix

**Decisions**

- Used comprehensive patch coverage rather than selective patching to prevent similar mismatches in other platform-specific crates going forward

**Status**

`holon-gpui` now compiles cleanly on `aarch64-apple-darwin` with only pre-existing warnings remaining.
